Tom's Photo Booth

A Combination of City and Nature Landscapes

Photography has been a significant part of my life for many years. I took related electives throughout the four years of high school, including AP 2-D Art and Design in which I focused on digital photography. It was one of the rare things that had brought me joy and kept me moving forward during those lonely days in the suburbia.

The bland life in the adolescent years definitively shaped my patterns of thinking, especially for my artistic investigations. I became obsessed with the city landscapes and their fusion with nature. Hence, I traveled whenever I had spare time. As time passed, my earnest passion for this art of light gradually diverted onto the path of building a sustainable lifetime hobby.

Up till this day, photography remains to be one of my sources of happiness. My camera is another pair of eyes capturing the astonishing moments of my life.
